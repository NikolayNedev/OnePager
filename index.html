Reasons I like photography

Photography is about simplicity.

It captures the beauty of life. Taking a photograph is taking a moment from our fast-paced lives a capturing it. Sometimes, we all need to take a moment and appreciate the small things in life, and photography allows me to do just that!

Another thing I love about being a hobbyist is that I have the freedom to create the pictures I want without any limitations. Without the worry of happy customers, I can take the time to experiment with all sorts of techniques and learn in my own time.

Photography is a hobby that you can make improvements quickly and see your progress easily. If you line up your photos next to each other from month to month, you will see those changes. In one year, those differences will be significant. Not only will you see how much better you are, seeing some of your favorites each month will give you that extra bit of motivation to keep clicking away.
